THANK YOU
FOR YOUR SUPPORT!

Congerville Fire & Rescue covers 5-1/2 miles of I-74 from mile marker 113 to the 118-1/2. Included in this stretch are two rest areas. Accidents and medical emergencies are common along this road. I-74 is by far the greatest hazard we as responders face on a continual basis. You don't have to volunteer to help us out. Do us a huge favor next time you come upon an emergency scene.
